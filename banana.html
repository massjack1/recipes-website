Chocolate Banana Milkshake
  • 10 Ingredients
  • 12 Minutes
  • 540 Calories
Ingredients
  • 5 frozen bananas
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 scoops chocolate powder
  • 2 scoops chocolate protein powder
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 Tbsp. vanilla extract
  • whipped cream (coconut)
  • chocolate syrup (or melted chocolate, dairy-free)
  • chocolate chips (dairy-free)
  • 1 cup of berries
Instructions Checklist
  1. Step 1

    Combine almond milk, frozen banana, chocolate powder, chocolate protein powder, vanilla ice cream, vanilla extract and chocolate syrup. Blend on high until smooth, about 1 minute.

  2. Step 2

    Add chocolate chips and any berries of choice in a glass jar. Pour in milkshake. Mix with spoon.

  3. Step 3

    Garnish with whipped cream.

  4. Step 4

    Serve & Enjoy.
