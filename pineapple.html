Easy Pineapple Dessert
  • 3 Ingredients
  • 30 Minutes
  • 130 Calories
Ingredients
  • 1 Can of crushed pineapple in juice
  • 100 ml vanilla flavor instant pudding
  • 1 cup whipped topping
Instructions Checklist
  1. Step 1

    In a medium glass, add 1 cup of crushed pineapple.

  2. Step 2

    Add the 100 ml vanilla flavor instant pudding and mix.

  3. Step 3

    Top the mix with 1 cup of whipped topping.

  4. Step 4

    Serve & Enjoy.
