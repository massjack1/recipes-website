Chocolate Chip Dream Bars
  • 14 Ingredients
  • 60 Minutes
  • 900 Calories
Ingredients
  • ⅓ cup butter, softened
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (Optional)
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 ½ tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 teaspoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons boiling water
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
Instructions Checklist
  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease a 9x13 inch pan.

  2. Step 2

    In a medium bowl, stir together 1 cup sugar, 3 cups flour, and baking powder. Mix in salt and cinnamon, if desired. Use a fork or pastry cutter to blend in the shortening and egg. Dough will be crumbly. Pat half of dough into the prepared pan.

  3. Step 3

    In another bowl, stir together the sugar and cornstarch. Gently mix in the blueberries. Sprinkle the blueberry mixture evenly over the crust. Crumble remaining dough over the berry layer.

  4. Step 4

    Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes, or until top is slightly brown. Cool completely before cutting into squares.

  5. Step 5

    To make the glaze: In a small saucepan over medium heat, mix together the cocoa, 4 teaspoons butter, and boiling water. Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, until thick and smooth. Remove from heat and stir in the confectioners' sugar. Drizzle over cooled bars before cutting into squares.
