Strawberry Milkshake
  • 5 Ingredients
  • 5 Minutes
  • 240 Calories
Ingredients
  • 12 strawberries (Large, Hulled)
  • 1 1/2 granulated sugar (Tablespoons)
  • 3/4 cup green tea (Acai, Chilled)
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
Instructions Checklist
  1. Step 1

    Combine coconut milk, strawberries, granulated sugar and a up of green tea. Blend on high until smooth, about 1 minute.

  2. Step 2

    Add Ice cubes in a glass jar. Pour in milkshake. Garnish with whipped cream.

  3. Step 3

    Take reserved strawberry and carefully cut at an angle but do not cut completely through. Place strawberry on the rim of the glass. Insert a straw and enjoy!

  4. Step 4

    Serve & Enjoy.
